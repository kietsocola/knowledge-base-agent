# Noi Dung Nop Hackathon

Cap nhat: 2026-03-29

## 1. Gioi thieu ngan

Ten de xuat san pham: **WellStudy AI Copilot**

Mo ta ngan:

WellStudy AI Copilot la nen tang tro ly hoc tap thong minh tich hop voi LMS, giup sinh vien hoi dap theo tai lieu mon hoc, theo doi tien trinh hoc tap, danh gia muc do hieu bai, phat hien som lo hong kien thuc va de xuat buoc hoc tiep theo. Khac voi chatbot hoi dap thong thuong, san pham duoc thiet ke theo huong `AI learning copilot`, ket hop RAG, tracking, classroom analytics, intervention workflow va planner flow nhieu buoc de ho tro ca nguoi hoc va giang vien.

## 2. Noi dung de dan vao form

### Tinh sang tao, doi moi va cong nghe

San pham noi bat o cho khong dung AI chi de tra loi cau hoi, ma xay dung mot mo hinh `AI learning copilot` cho moi truong hoc tap so. He thong ket hop nhieu lop cong nghe hien dai: truy xuat tri thuc theo ngu canh mon hoc bang RAG, citation de tang tinh minh bach, tracking theo concept, luu learning events theo thoi gian, dashboard hoc tap, canh bao som va planner flow cho phien hoc tiep theo. Nhờ đó, moi phien chat khong chi tao ra cau tra loi, ma con tao ra du lieu hoc tap de phan tich va ho tro sau nay.

Diem doi moi cua san pham nam o kha nang chuyen tu `chatbot hoi dap` sang `he thong dong hanh hoc tap`. Sau khi sinh vien hoi dap, he thong tu dong tong hop muc do manh, diem con yeu, concept can tap trung, nhip hoc gan day va de xuat hanh dong tiep theo. O cap lop hoc, dashboard giup nhin ra concept nghen chung, nhom sinh vien can chu y va xu huong tham gia hoc tap. O cap ca nhan, he thong de xuat intervention som va study plan nhieu buoc voi vai tro `Diagnosis Agent`, `Planner Agent` va `Tutor Agent`, giup cau chuyen multi-agent tro nen thuyet phuc, dung tinh chat cua mot he thong ho tro hoc tap thong minh.

Ve cong nghe, du an su dung Next.js App Router, PostgreSQL, Drizzle ORM, pgvector, OpenAI API va parser PDF runtime o muc san sang demo. Kien truc nay phu hop dieu kien trien khai tai Viet Nam vi stack pho bien, de mo rong, de tim nhan su va co the phu len LMS hien co ma khong bat buoc nha truong thay doi toan bo he thong. Giai phap cung phu hop voi thuc te hoc lieu tai Viet Nam, noi tai lieu thuong phan tan, sinh vien can cong cu hoi dap 24/7, con giang vien can them goc nhin de phat hien nhom hoc sinh dang gap kho khan.

Noi cach khac, tinh sang tao cua san pham khong nam o viec “co chat voi AI”, ma nam o viec bien moi tuong tac hoc tap thanh du lieu co cau truc, phan tich duoc, theo doi duoc va chuyen hoa thanh hanh dong ho tro cu the.

### Tinh ung dung

Tinh ung dung cua san pham rat cao vi bai toan goc la bai toan that trong giao duc: sinh vien khong biet hoi ai, tai lieu qua nhieu, kho tim dung noi dung, hoc xong khong biet minh dang yeu o dau, con giang vien kho theo doi het muc do hien tai cua tung nguoi hoc. WellStudy AI Copilot giai quyet dong thoi cac van de nay bang mot giao dien quen thuoc, de su dung va co the tich hop len quy trinh hoc tap hien co.

Sinh vien co the vao he thong qua LMS demo, chon mon hoc, chat theo tai lieu, nhan cau tra loi co citation, xem evaluation sau phien hoc, theo doi dashboard hoc tap, nhan canh bao som va nhan study plan cho buoc tiep theo. Giang vien co the xem dashboard lop hoc de biet concept nao dang la diem nghen chung, sinh vien nao can can thiep, va xu huong hoc tap cua lop theo thoi gian. Vi vay, san pham khong chi phuc vu nhu cau tu hoc cua sinh vien ma con ho tro ra quyet dinh giao duc o cap lop hoc.

Mo hinh nay co kha nang trien khai rong vi co the dong vai tro la lop AI phu len LMS hien co. Nha truong khong can thay doi toan bo he thong quan ly hoc tap, ma co the tich hop them mot lop dong hanh thong minh. Cac truong dai hoc, trung tam dao tao, he thong hoc truc tuyen, don vi boi duong ky nang, thậm chi doanh nghiep co nhu cau dao tao noi bo deu co the ung dung.

Ve phuong dien kinh doanh, san pham co the mo rong theo huong B2B/B2G: ban cho truong hoc, trung tam giao duc, don vi dao tao, hoac cac to chuc can he thong tro ly hoc tap noi bo. Gia tri mang lai khong chi la tra loi nhanh hon, ma la tang nang suat day va hoc, cai thien chat luong tu hoc va giam chi phi ho tro lap lai. Day la mot bai toan co thi truong ro rang va co kha nang phat trien thanh san pham lau dai sau hackathon.

### Tinh hieu qua

San pham tao ra hieu qua o ca ba cap: sinh vien, giang vien va he thong giao duc.

O cap sinh vien, he thong giup rut ngan thoi gian tim kiem tai lieu, giam su phan tan thong tin, tang toc do tu hoc va tao cam giac hoc co dinh huong. Thay vi doc nhieu tai lieu roi van mo ho, sinh vien co the hoi truc tiep, duoc giai thich theo ngu canh mon hoc, duoc biet minh manh o dau, yeu o dau, va can lam gi tiep theo. Evaluation, intervention va planner flow giup nguoi hoc khong bi mac ket trong vong lap “hoi xong de do”.

O cap giang vien, san pham giam bot cac cau hoi lap lai, giup phat hien som nhom sinh vien dang gap kho khan, va cung cap goc nhin nhanh ve concept nghen cua ca lop. Dashboard lop hoc va intervention alerts cho phep giang vien chuyen tu mo hinh phan ung thu cong sang mo hinh ho tro co chu y, tiet kiem thoi gian ma van nang cao chat luong huong dan.

O cap he thong, san pham gop phan chuyen doi cach van hanh ho tro hoc tap tu thu cong sang co du lieu va co AI ho tro. Khi du lieu hoi dap, evaluation va learning events duoc luu lai co cau truc, nha truong co the tung buoc xay dung hoc tap ca nhan hoa, canh bao som, thong ke tien trinh va ra quyet dinh dua tren minh chung cu the thay vi cam tinh. Day la gia tri rat lon trong boi canh chuyen doi so giao duc.

Hieu qua cua san pham khong chi o “tra loi nhanh”, ma o viec giup hoc dung thu tu, can thiệp dung luc va giup nguoi hoc thay duoc su tien bo cua chinh minh.

### Tiem nang phat trien

San pham co tiem nang phat trien tot vi da co mot nen tang ky thuat ro rang va mot roadmap phat trien thuyet phuc. Phien ban hien tai da co: RAG theo course, session history, evaluation, tracking theo concept, learning timeline, dashboard ca nhan, dashboard lop hoc, intervention workflow va planner flow. Nghia la du an da co “xuong song” cua mot nen tang learning copilot, khong con chi la y tuong.

Tu nen tang nay, huong phat trien tiep theo rat ro:

- nang cap tu workflow-level multi-agent len runtime multi-agent orchestration
- them notification va escalation workflow den giang vien that
- chuan hoa taxonomy concept va rubric danh gia theo tung mon
- them analytics sau hon cho cap khoa, cap truong, cap don vi dao tao
- hoan thien tich hop LTI production va cac he thong LMS pho bien

Ve doi ngu, du an da su dung stack pho bien, de tuyen bo sung nhan su va de nang cap sau hackathon. Ve san pham, day la loai bai toan co tinh ben vung cao, giai quyet nhu cau that, co kha nang nhan duoc su quan tam tu truong hoc, to chuc giao duc va don vi dau tu trong edtech. Neu duoc tiep tuc phat trien, san pham co the tro thanh mot nen tang AI dong hanh hoc tap va phan tich hoc tap thong minh cho thi truong Viet Nam.

### Tinh cong dong

Tinh cong dong la mot diem manh rat ro cua san pham. Trong boi canh giao duc tai Viet Nam, khong phai sinh vien nao cung co dieu kien hoc them, tim gia su, hay duoc giai dap kip thoi khi gap van de trong qua trinh hoc. WellStudy AI Copilot mo rong kha nang tiep can ho tro hoc tap 24/7, giup giam khoang cach giua nhom sinh vien co nhieu dieu kien va nhom sinh vien co it dieu kien hon.

He thong dac biet co y nghia voi:

- sinh vien o khu vuc kho tiep can nguon bo tro hoc tap chat luong cao
- nguoi hoc can tu hoc nhieu nhung thieu nguoi dong hanh
- lop hoc dong, giang vien kho theo sat tung ca nhan
- moi truong hoc co nhieu tai lieu nhung thieu cong cu huong dan hoc dung cach

Gia tri cong dong cua san pham nam o cho no khong thay the giao vien, ma lam cho nguon luc ho tro giao duc duoc phan bo tot hon. AI xu ly nhung cau hoi lap lai, tong hop dau hieu hoc tap, va giup phat hien som nguoi hoc dang tut lai. Nhờ vậy, nguoi day co the danh thoi gian cho nhung truong hop can su ho tro that su. Day la mot huong phat trien co y nghia xa hoi, gop phan nang cao chat luong hoc tap, mo rong co hoi tiep can tri thuc va thu hep chenh lech giao duc.
